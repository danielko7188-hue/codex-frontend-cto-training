# Reference Entry

- Name: Generic AI startup template
- URL or source: recurring pattern observed across many template libraries
- Product type: anti-pattern
- Bucket: hate
- Date reviewed: 2026-04-06

## First impression

- Strong: can look modern for a few seconds
- Weak: forgettable, low-trust, interchangeable, usually weak on product fit

## What to borrow

- very little

## What not to borrow

- purple-on-white gradients by default
- card soup
- vague AI copy
- hero sections with no proof
- dashboard cards pasted into marketing pages
- decorative glow instead of real hierarchy

## Why this matters

- The founder already dislikes this direction, and it is one of the most common ways UI starts feeling generic.

## Reusable lesson

- If the design could belong to any random AI tool, it is probably not strong enough.
