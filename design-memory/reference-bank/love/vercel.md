# Reference Entry

- Name: Vercel
- URL or source: https://vercel.com/
- Product type: developer platform / marketing + product
- Bucket: love
- Date reviewed: 2026-04-06

## First impression

- Strong: restrained, premium, technically credible, strong typography, excellent pacing
- Weak: can be too austere for warmer local-service brands if copied too literally

## What to borrow

- typography: sharp, confident, high-contrast hierarchy
- layout: disciplined section pacing with one dominant idea at a time
- spacing: generous but never sleepy
- CTA treatment: clear primary action with quiet secondary action
- trust/proof: proof appears early without clutter
- motion: subtle, polished, and supportive

## What not to borrow

- the exact coldness for businesses that need more human warmth
- platform-specific visual language when the product is not technical

## Why this matters

- It proves premium can come from discipline rather than decoration.

## Reusable lesson

- Better typography and spacing usually outperform adding more visual devices.
