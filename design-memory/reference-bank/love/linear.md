# Reference Entry

- Name: Linear
- URL or source: https://linear.app/
- Product type: SaaS / internal product feel
- Bucket: love
- Date reviewed: 2026-04-06

## First impression

- Strong: calm, dense enough, serious, product-led, highly intentional
- Weak: if copied directly, some consumer-facing products may feel too cold or too spare

## What to borrow

- typography: compact, confident, clean
- layout: main workspace dominates, chrome stays quiet
- spacing: tight but readable
- CTA treatment: understated but obvious
- trust/proof: competence comes from consistency and control
- motion: restrained and almost invisible

## What not to borrow

- over-minimizing warmth for service or lifestyle brands
- reducing helpful proof in marketing contexts

## Why this matters

- It is one of the strongest references for operational SaaS restraint.

## Reusable lesson

- Calm product UI comes from hierarchy and density discipline, not from adding more cards or color.
