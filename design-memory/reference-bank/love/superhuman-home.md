## First impression

- Strong: bold but still polished, product-first, AI-native without feeling chaotic
- Weak: the energy level can be too high for sober enterprise or service products

## What to borrow

- hero energy: the page has motion and ambition without losing the product story
- product packaging: multiple products still feel like one coherent suite
- AI framing: assistance is shown inside actual work, not as a floating gimmick
- visual rhythm: large proof moments are balanced with clear product modules
- brand confidence: expressive, but still easy to scan

## What not to borrow

- launch-style energy when the product should feel calm or formal
- visual intensity that overwhelms trust and clarity

## Why this matters

- It is a useful bold-launch and calm-AI crossover reference because it shows energy without turning into noise.

## Reusable lesson

- A bold page still needs one clear product story and concrete workflow examples or it will just feel loud.
