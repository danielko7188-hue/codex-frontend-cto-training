# Reference Entry

- Name: ServiceTitan
- URL or source: https://www.servicetitan.com/
- Product type: home and commercial services software
- Bucket: love

## First impression

- Strong: service-led trust, concrete ROI proof, vertical specificity, strong demo motion
- Weak: can get heavy and form-driven if copied without restraint

## What to borrow

- vertical fit: the page clearly speaks the language of the trades
- proof: revenue, cash-flow, and productivity outcomes are concrete and repeated
- trust: case studies, reviews, and vertical segmentation show real-world credibility
- conversion: demo flow is visible and never ambiguous
- messaging: software value is tied to business outcomes, not generic efficiency claims

## What not to borrow

- oversized multi-step lead capture for small products
- too many category links when the business is simpler

## Why this matters

- It is a strong warm-service reference because it balances human trust, operational clarity, and obvious business value.

## Reusable lesson

- Service businesses feel more premium when the UI proves it understands the operator's day-to-day economics, not just the brand style.
