# Reference Entry

- Name: One Medical
- URL or source: https://www.onemedical.com/
- Product type: healthcare / membership primary care
- Bucket: love
- Date reviewed: 2026-04-07

## First impression

- Strong: calm, humane, low-friction, reassuring without feeling old-fashioned
- Weak: can feel too soft if copied onto products that need stronger operational authority

## What to borrow

- tone: healthcare is presented in plain language with emotional relief, not just clinical feature lists
- trust: insurance, locations, providers, and care modes are surfaced early
- friction reduction: getting care, membership cost, and what the app can do are all easy to find
- reassurance: the page lowers anxiety by making speed, coverage, and convenience concrete
- hierarchy: the promise is simple and the proof stays close to the claim

## What not to borrow

- overly gentle tone for products that need urgency or stronger business energy
- healthcare softness without matching operational specifics

## Why this matters

- It is a strong healthcare reference because it balances warmth, clarity, and trust in a category where anxiety is high.

## Reusable lesson

- Healthcare products feel better when the interface reduces uncertainty first and explains logistics in plain English.
