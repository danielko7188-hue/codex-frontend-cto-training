## What This Teaches

- how premium websites can be translated into DESIGN.md instructions for agents
- how to express visual systems in plain English and markdown
- how to reuse style direction across threads and projects without screenshots
- how DESIGN.md can complement AGENTS.md in an agent-driven workflow

## What To Inspect First

- design-md directory
- README usage instructions
- individual product DESIGN.md files
- preview.html and preview-dark.html patterns

## What Not To Copy Blindly

- treating one borrowed DESIGN.md as perfect for every product
- copying brand-specific choices without adapting them to the founder taste profile
- confusing inspiration with permission to clone a product

## Why This Matters

A strong bridge between visual references and agent-executable design direction.

## Reusable Lesson

A good DESIGN.md can carry design intent across projects, but it works best when adapted to the product and founder taste.
