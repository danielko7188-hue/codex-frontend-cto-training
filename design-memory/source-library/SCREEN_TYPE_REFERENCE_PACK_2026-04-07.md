# Screen Type Reference Pack 2026-04-07

This file maps common screen types to stronger real-world references.

Its purpose is structure.

Style lanes answer "how should this feel?"

Business-type packs answer "what kind of company is this?"

Screen-type packs answer "what job is this specific screen doing?"

Use this pack after choosing the business type and style lane.

## Homepage / Landing Page

- Primary anchors: [Vercel](../reference-bank/love/vercel.md), [Linear](../reference-bank/love/linear.md), [ServiceTitan](../reference-bank/love/servicetitan-home.md)
- Support anchors: [Notion Sites](../reference-bank/love/notion-sites.md), [Mercury](../reference-bank/love/mercury.md)
- Best for: first-touch pages, product marketing, founder sites, lead-gen homepages
- Borrow: fast promise, early proof, clean section pacing, one obvious next action
- Watch out for: card soup, vague hero copy, decorative emptiness

## Pricing

- Primary anchors: [Stripe](../reference-bank/love/stripe-home.md), [Notion](../reference-bank/love/notion-home.md), [Vercel](../reference-bank/love/vercel-home.md)
- Support anchors: [Mercury](../reference-bank/love/mercury-home.md), [Ramp](../reference-bank/love/ramp-home.md)
- Best for: plan comparison, self-serve SaaS, sales-assisted pricing, upgrade pages
- Borrow: pricing clarity, trust around billing, good FAQ placement, clear plan differentiation
- Watch out for: hiding the real decision behind too much copy

## Dashboard / Workspace Home

- Primary anchors: [Retool](../reference-bank/love/retool-home.md), [Cal Teams](../reference-bank/love/cal-teams.md), [Intercom Inbox](../reference-bank/love/intercom-inbox.md)
- Support anchors: [Shopify Polaris](../reference-bank/love/shopify-polaris.md), [Cloudflare](../reference-bank/love/cloudflare-home.md)
- Best for: product homes, operator consoles, team workspaces, admin surfaces
- Borrow: density with hierarchy, useful default views, visible next actions, real task surfaces
- Watch out for: oversized cards and weak task priority

## Onboarding / Setup Flow

- Primary anchors: [Notion Sites](../reference-bank/love/notion-sites.md), [Airbnb Host Homes](../reference-bank/love/airbnb-host-homes.md), [One Medical](../reference-bank/love/one-medical-home.md)
- Support anchors: [ServiceTitan](../reference-bank/love/servicetitan-home.md), [Vercel](../reference-bank/love/vercel.md)
- Best for: first-run experiences, account setup, supplier onboarding, healthcare intake, guided configuration
- Borrow: low-friction sequencing, reassurance, plain-English steps, anxiety reduction
- Watch out for: too many fields too early or mystery progress

## Chat / AI Conversation Surface

- Primary anchors: [ChatGPT Overview](../reference-bank/love/chatgpt-overview.md), [Claude Code](../reference-bank/love/claude-code.md), [Notion AI](../reference-bank/love/notion-ai.md)
- Support anchors: [Superhuman](../reference-bank/love/superhuman-home.md), [OpenAI Codex](../reference-bank/love/openai-codex.md)
- Best for: chat copilots, AI assistants, side panels, AI-first workflows
- Borrow: calm message hierarchy, visible actions, inspectable output, confidence through editability
- Watch out for: turning every task into a chat bubble when structure would help more

## Approval Workflow / Review Surface

- Primary anchors: [Claude Code](../reference-bank/love/claude-code.md), [Retool](../reference-bank/love/retool-home.md), [Cloudflare](../reference-bank/love/cloudflare-home.md)
- Support anchors: [OpenAI Codex](../reference-bank/love/openai-codex.md), [Intercom Inbox](../reference-bank/love/intercom-inbox.md)
- Best for: human-in-the-loop AI tools, compliance review, operations approvals, change review
- Borrow: clear before/after state, actions with consequences, traceability, strong primary decisions
- Watch out for: pretty output without control, history, or verification

## Settings / Account Management

- Primary anchors: [Shopify Polaris](../reference-bank/love/shopify-polaris.md), [Cloudflare](../reference-bank/love/cloudflare-home.md), [Cal Teams](../reference-bank/love/cal-teams.md)
- Support anchors: [Mercury](../reference-bank/love/mercury.md), [Stripe](../reference-bank/love/stripe-home.md)
- Best for: account settings, team management, billing settings, security and preferences
- Borrow: strong grouping, plain labels, calm density, low-error interaction design
- Watch out for: burying dangerous actions or over-styling boring but important tasks

## Lead Form / Contact Conversion

- Primary anchors: [ServiceTitan](../reference-bank/love/servicetitan-home.md), [Airbnb Host Homes](../reference-bank/love/airbnb-host-homes.md), [One Medical](../reference-bank/love/one-medical-home.md)
- Support anchors: [Notion Sites](../reference-bank/love/notion-sites.md), [Mercury](../reference-bank/love/mercury-home.md)
- Best for: demo requests, quote forms, service inquiries, membership signup, sales capture
- Borrow: short steps, visible trust, clear privacy expectations, low-friction input flow
- Watch out for: turning form completion into a chore

## How To Use This Pack

1. Choose the business type with [BUSINESS_TYPE_REFERENCE_PACK_2026-04-07.md](./BUSINESS_TYPE_REFERENCE_PACK_2026-04-07.md).
2. Choose the style lane with [STYLE_SPECTRUM_2026-04-07.md](./STYLE_SPECTRUM_2026-04-07.md).
3. Choose the screen type from this file.
4. Read 2-3 references from the business type, 2-3 from the style lane, and 2-3 from the screen type.
5. Write a one-sentence screen thesis before coding.

## Core Rule

Good UX/UI is not only about brand taste.

It is also about giving each screen the right structure for its job.
