# Minimalist Premium

## Best For

- polished landing pages
- founder-led SaaS
- premium B2B products
- trust-first product marketing

## Core Feel

Calm, restrained, expensive, controlled.

## Signals

- strong typography
- quiet surfaces
- sparse but intentional accents
- generous spacing without emptiness
- proof integrated cleanly, not loudly

## Avoid

- card soup
- too much blur or glass
- empty luxury theater with no proof
