# Bold Launch

## Best For

- product launches
- campaigns
- attention-first landing pages
- products competing in noisy categories

## Core Feel

Energetic, directional, high-contrast, memorable.

## Signals

- stronger hero move
- bolder composition
- more aggressive contrast between sections
- selective motion and visual punch

## Avoid

- using it for trust-first compliance or service sites
- bright noise without structure
- shouting across every section
