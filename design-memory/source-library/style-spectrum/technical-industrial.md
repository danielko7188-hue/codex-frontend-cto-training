# Technical Industrial

## Best For

- manufacturing
- hardware
- supply chain
- infrastructure
- export, logistics, or systems businesses

## Core Feel

Precise, engineered, serious, operational.

## Signals

- firmer grid logic
- sharper edges and denser proof
- diagrams, specs, process, and evidence matter
- materials and production feel real, not decorative

## Avoid

- soft lifestyle SaaS styling
- over-rounding everything
- overusing premium polish where technical proof should lead
