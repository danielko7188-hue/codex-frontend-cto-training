# Enterprise Trust

## Best For

- operational products
- compliance-heavy workflows
- B2B dashboards
- infrastructure and service businesses

## Core Feel

Reliable, clear, procedural, credible.

## Signals

- clearer labels than marketing sites
- visible process
- explicit proof and capability statements
- conservative color use
- lower decoration, higher clarity

## Avoid

- hype-first hero sections
- cute consumer styling
- vague claims with no operational detail
