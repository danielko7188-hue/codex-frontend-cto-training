# Style Spectrum Reference Pack 2026-04-07

This file maps each style lane to stronger real-world references.

Its purpose is to give Codex more range.

Do not read all of these every time. Pick the lane first, then study 2-4 anchors that fit the product.

## Minimalist Premium

- Primary anchors: [Vercel](../reference-bank/love/vercel.md), [Linear](../reference-bank/love/linear.md), [Mercury](../reference-bank/love/mercury.md)
- Support anchors: [Apple Design](../reference-bank/love/apple-design.md), [Notion Sites](../reference-bank/love/notion-sites.md)
- Best for: premium SaaS, modern service software, calm founder-led products
- Watch out for: turning premium into bland

## Enterprise Trust

- Primary anchors: [Cloudflare](../reference-bank/love/cloudflare-home.md), [Stripe](../reference-bank/love/stripe-home.md), [Shopify Polaris](../reference-bank/love/shopify-polaris.md)
- Support anchors: [ServiceTitan](../reference-bank/love/servicetitan-home.md), [Ramp](../reference-bank/love/ramp-home.md)
- Best for: B2B infrastructure, operations software, compliance-heavy products
- Watch out for: corporate heaviness without strong proof

## Editorial Luxury

- Primary anchors: [Apple Design](../reference-bank/love/apple-design.md), [Notion Sites](../reference-bank/love/notion-sites.md), [Framer](../reference-bank/love/framer-home.md)
- Support anchors: [Notion](../reference-bank/love/notion-home.md), [Figma](../reference-bank/love/figma-home.md)
- Best for: high-perception brands, design-led launches, story-first pages
- Watch out for: becoming sparse and vague

## Technical Industrial

- Primary anchors: [Flexport](../reference-bank/love/flexport-home.md), [Cloudflare](../reference-bank/love/cloudflare-home.md)
- Support anchors: [ServiceTitan](../reference-bank/love/servicetitan-home.md), [Retool](../reference-bank/love/retool-home.md)
- Best for: logistics, manufacturing, infra, platforms with real operating surfaces
- Watch out for: jargon and navigation overload

## Warm Service

- Primary anchors: [ServiceTitan](../reference-bank/love/servicetitan-home.md), [Mercury](../reference-bank/love/mercury.md)
- Support anchors: [Notion Sites](../reference-bank/love/notion-sites.md), [Ramp](../reference-bank/love/ramp-home.md)
- Best for: service businesses, trusted advisors, local and vertical software with human relationships
- Watch out for: trying to sound too corporate or too playful

## Bold Launch

- Primary anchors: [Superhuman](../reference-bank/love/superhuman-home.md), [v0](../reference-bank/love/v0-home.md), [Framer](../reference-bank/love/framer-home.md)

## Calm AI Workspace

- Primary anchors: [Claude Code](../reference-bank/love/claude-code.md), [ChatGPT Overview](../reference-bank/love/chatgpt-overview.md), [Notion AI](../reference-bank/love/notion-ai.md)

## Operator Density

## How To Use This Pack

1. Pick the lane with [STYLE_SPECTRUM_2026-04-07.md](./STYLE_SPECTRUM_2026-04-07.md).
2. Use [DESIGN_DIRECTION_SELECTOR.md](../playbooks/DESIGN_DIRECTION_SELECTOR.md) to lock the primary lane, support lane, and anti-lane.
3. Read 2-4 references from the matching lane.
4. State what to borrow and what not to borrow before coding.

## Core Rule

Range is not about adding more visual noise.

Range means knowing which product should feel:

- calmer
- more operational
- more service-led
- more launch-driven
- more editorial
- more dense and tool-like
