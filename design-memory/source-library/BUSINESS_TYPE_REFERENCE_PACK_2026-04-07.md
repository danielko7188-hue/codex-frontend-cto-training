# Business Type Reference Pack 2026-04-07

This file maps common business types to stronger real-world references.

Its purpose is product fit.

Style lanes answer "how should this feel?"

Business-type packs answer "what kind of company is this, and what does that audience need to trust quickly?"

Use this pack after choosing a style lane, not instead of it.

## Fintech

- Primary anchors: [Mercury](../reference-bank/love/mercury.md), [Ramp](../reference-bank/love/ramp-home.md), [Stripe](../reference-bank/love/stripe-home.md)
- Support anchors: [Cloudflare](../reference-bank/love/cloudflare-home.md), [Vercel](../reference-bank/love/vercel.md)
- Best for: banking, payments, spend management, finance operations, money movement
- Borrow: restrained authority, trust signals, compliance-friendly calm, numbers-first proof
- Watch out for: looking sterile or jargon-heavy

## Healthcare

- Primary anchors: [One Medical](../reference-bank/love/one-medical-home.md)
- Support anchors: [Apple Design](../reference-bank/love/apple-design.md), [Notion Sites](../reference-bank/love/notion-sites.md)
- Best for: telehealth, patient portals, care coordination, wellness products that need trust
- Borrow: reassurance, plain-English logistics, gentle but credible hierarchy, friction reduction
- Watch out for: softness without enough practical information

## Marketplaces

- Primary anchors: [Faire](../reference-bank/love/faire-home.md), [Airbnb Host Homes](../reference-bank/love/airbnb-host-homes.md)
- Support anchors: [Zillow](https://www.zillow.com/)
- Best for: supply-and-demand platforms, seller onboarding, discovery-led products, commerce ecosystems
- Borrow: discovery paths, risk-reduction policies, onboarding clarity, two-sided trust
- Watch out for: dumping too many categories or search surfaces onto a product that is still simple

## Local Services

- Primary anchors: [ServiceTitan](../reference-bank/love/servicetitan-home.md)
- Support anchors: [Notion Sites](../reference-bank/love/notion-sites.md), [Mercury](../reference-bank/love/mercury.md)
- Best for: contractor sites, home services, agencies, service-led software, lead-gen pages
- Borrow: business-outcome proof, clear contact paths, trust signals, lower-friction forms
- Watch out for: trendy visuals outranking clarity and trust

## AI Dashboards

- Primary anchors: [Retool](../reference-bank/love/retool-home.md), [Claude Code](../reference-bank/love/claude-code.md), [ChatGPT Overview](../reference-bank/love/chatgpt-overview.md)
- Support anchors: [OpenAI Codex](../reference-bank/love/openai-codex.md), [Notion AI](../reference-bank/love/notion-ai.md), [Superhuman](../reference-bank/love/superhuman-home.md)
- Best for: AI workspaces, copilots, agent consoles, review-and-approve systems, tool-heavy AI products
- Borrow: inspectable output, real work surfaces, visible state, confidence through control
- Watch out for: chat-only UX when the task actually needs structure

## How To Use This Pack

1. Choose the business type first.
2. Choose the style lane with [STYLE_SPECTRUM_2026-04-07.md](./STYLE_SPECTRUM_2026-04-07.md).
3. Read 2-3 references from this business-type pack and 2-3 from the style-spectrum reference pack.
4. Write down:
   - what the audience needs to trust
   - what proof they expect
   - what friction must be removed
5. Only then start coding.

## Core Rule

Products fail when they borrow the right aesthetic from the wrong category.

A fintech page, healthcare page, marketplace, local service site, and AI dashboard should not all solve trust the same way.
