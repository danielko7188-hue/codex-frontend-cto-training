## What To Inspect First

- template categories by use case
- starter repos linked from the hub

## What Not To Copy Blindly

- treating a starter as the final product direction
- pulling in a template because it is convenient instead of because it fits the business

## Why This Matters

A strong source for choosing better starting points before custom frontend work begins.

## Reusable Lesson

Good starter selection can save time, but only if the structure matches the product job.
