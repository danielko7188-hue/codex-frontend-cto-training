## Snapshot

- Bucket: templates
- Focus: modern SaaS product skeleton
- Product type: SaaS starter kit

## What This Teaches

- how a serious SaaS starter organizes public and authenticated surfaces
- billing, auth, and product shell boundaries
- good default route structure for a real software business
- how to begin with production-oriented scaffolding instead of toy structure

## What To Inspect First

- app structure
- marketing versus app surfaces
- dashboard routes
- shared components and styles

## What Not To Copy Blindly

- assuming starter business logic equals your product strategy
- keeping generic SaaS copy or layout choices after the initial setup

## Why This Matters

Useful when a project needs a real SaaS foundation instead of an isolated page or demo.

## Reusable Lesson

A good SaaS starter reduces setup drag, but the product still needs its own point of view.
